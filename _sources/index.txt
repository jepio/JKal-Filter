=======================================
Welcome to JKalFilter's documentation!
=======================================

A Kalman Filter library written entirely in Python. JKalFilter is a modular framework
consisting of building blocks neccessary to use Kalman Filters for various
applications, not limited to but including track fitting in High Energy Physics.
